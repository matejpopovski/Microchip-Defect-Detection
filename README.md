# Sample Defect Types

## Crack
<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/crack_sample.png" width="220"/>
</p>

Crack defects represent fractured or split regions on the microchip surface and are often characterized by thin irregular line structures.

---

## Hole
<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/hole_sample.png" width="220"/>
</p>

Hole defects contain visible missing material or puncture-like regions that interrupt the surface continuity.

---

## Normal
<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/normal_sample.png" width="220"/>
</p>

Normal samples contain no visible defects and represent clean manufacturing conditions.

---

## Rust
<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/rust_sample.png" width="220"/>
</p>

Rust defects include corrosion-like texture degradation and irregular discoloration patterns.

---

## Scratch
<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/scratch_sample.png" width="220"/>
</p>

Scratch defects appear as surface abrasions or linear texture disruptions caused during manufacturing or handling.

---
